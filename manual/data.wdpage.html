The purpose of the attribute is splits the children of the element into groups and display only a certain group:

data-wd-page=page:size

The page attribute is required and has the same purpose as the same-named attribute of the HTML.page object.

The size attribute is required and has the same purpose as the same-named attribute of the HTML.page object.

See the method HTML.page to understand the source of its operation!

Target:

  • Child elements.

Sensitivity:

  • When loading the document.
  • When defining the attribute on the element (See the HTML.data method).
  • After external data loading procedures (repeat/load).

Example:

Sets Attribute:

data-wd-page= 0:0 | 0:5 | 1:5 | -1:5 | -2:5

data-wd-page= 0:4 | 1:4 | 2:4 | -1:4 | -2:4 | -3:4

Result:

  1. {{nominativo}}
  2. {{genitivo}}
