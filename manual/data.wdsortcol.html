The purpose of the attribute sorts columns of tables (table). The attribute must be contained in the elements td or th, children of tr, child of thead. When you click on the elements that contain the attribute, the respective column will be sorted in ascending order (tbody). When you click on the element again, the sort order reverses:

data-wd-sort-col=

See the method HTML.sort to understand the source of its operation!

Target:

  • tbody children.

Sensitivity:

  • When the element receives a click.
